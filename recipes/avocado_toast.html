Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast! Toast with avocado! My favorite breakfast item to have with my girlfriend. Ingredients:

  • Avocado
  • Bread
  • Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Cut avocado in half
  3. Remove avocado contents with spoon
  4. Mash contents
  5. Sprinkle everything bagel sesasoning
